Discover / connect / develop

The network is built to help big ideas gather the people and support they need.

The site should feel useful to creators and inspiring to outside viewers. These sections make the value legible without slipping into startup or nonprofit cliches.

Discover

Curated project visibility for work that often sits between disciplines and gets missed by standard platforms.

Connect

Pathways for artists, architects, engineers, fabricators, curators, producers, and supporters to find one another through projects.

Develop

Support structures for collaboration, funding context, proposal development, and future AI-assisted planning tools.

Collaboration in action

Open roles make the network feel alive.

The platform should preview real opportunities for participation rather than only talking about community in the abstract.

Grant Research Lead

Help map aligned arts, ecology, and public-space opportunities for Resonance and related projects.

Structural Advisor

Review shell, canopy, and prefabricated systems for feasibility, sequencing, and material optimization.

Site Partner

Provide a museum, residency, festival, or civic context where a project can be tested, installed, or evolved.
